CrystalCollector!

Instructions


  • A random number will be assigned to the large diamond in the center.
  • The four gemstones below Ruby, Saphire, Emerald, and Topaz all will be assigned an unknown number.
  • By clicking on one of the gemstones, the value of that gemstone is added to your number.
  • The objective of the game is to click the right amount of gemstones to match the number assigned to the diamond.
  • Match the numbers and... You Win!... Go Over... No bueno...
  • PS...The numbers change every time!
